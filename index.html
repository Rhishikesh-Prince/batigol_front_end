About Batigol

Batigol is a reputed sports apparel manufacturer and retailer based in Doha, Qatar. Established in 2013 under the leadership of Mr. Younis Darwish, Batigol specializes in designing and producing customized sportswear including jerseys, training kits, uniforms and accessories.

Our in-house production facility allows us to maintain high quality standards and deliver products with faster turnaround time.
